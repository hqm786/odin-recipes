Mojito

Description

A very refreshing drink for hot summer days

Ingredients

  • Mint
  • Lime
  • Sugar
  • Club Soda/li>

Steps

  1. Place mint leaves and 1 lime wedge into a sturdy glass. Use a muddler and crush to release mint oils and lime juice.
  2. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Fill the glass almost to the top with ice. Fill the glass with club soda.
  4. Stir, taste, and add more sugar if desired.
